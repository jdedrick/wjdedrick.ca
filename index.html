A little about this site.

wjdedrick.ca is intended as a simplified web cv, most specifically to highlight my professional history. Through constant self analysis I hope to discover opportunities which will strengthen communication and intellectual challenges by working with others in creating meaningful user experiences through today's IT resources.

I have a robust history in various areas of development, which began (many years ago) with basic web design (coming from animation), and gradually, tackling a fear of coding, I delved the more programatic aspect of things, which lead to server side development. The focus on server side language has been largely PHP, with MYSQL. Although my attention (professionally) has been in the backend for some time, I have always had a keen interest in the front-end / user facing side of development, and find myself at home in the (Chrome) browser developer tools for debugging and testing.

As of late, I've been loving Javascript, and as such, have been playing with Backbonejs, Angularjs, Nodejs, and Express, while familiarizing myself with javascript design patterns. I also have a desire to become much more familiar with the user experience aspect of the industry. I have zero ego, and value every opportunity to learn from other developers who are generous with their knowledge.
